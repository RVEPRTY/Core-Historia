Core Historia is a collaborative digital organization made up of researchers, programmers, analysts, journalists, and skilled individuals united by a shared goal: building a safer and more peaceful online society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote awareness, documentation, and education surrounding harmful online activity. Through responsible research and community cooperation, we work to expose malicious behavior and help protect individuals from digital threats.

By joining Core Historia, members gain access to information resources, news updates, educational documents, discussions, and a community of like-minded people committed to integrity and responsibility.

Join Us

We welcome motivated individuals of all backgrounds. No experience is required — only discipline, respect, and commitment to our mission.

Skills that help us grow include research, writing, design, moderation, leadership, and cybersecurity interest.

Rules & Conduct

These laws are to be strictly followed by all members and will rarely be permitted to be bypassed. A decided consequence will be applied to any violation. Consequences may be stacked. The highest authorities may create exceptions when necessary.

  • Legality: All activities must be conducted in accordance with regional laws.
  • Distribution: Leaking classified organizational information is prohibited. Sending gore or pornographic material is not permitted.
  • Collection: All information must be obtained using proper and legal methods.
  • Discrimination: Discrimination against others is not permitted.
  • Defamation: Defamation of others is not permitted.
  • Doxxing: Doxxing others is strictly prohibited.
  • Scamming: Scamming is not permitted.
  • Entrapment: Entrapment is not permitted.
  • Spying: Spying on the organization is not permitted.
  • Participation: Individuals involved in criminal or rival organizations are not permitted to participate.
  • Ragebaiting: Ragebaiting is not permitted.
  • Linking: Malicious links or downloads are not permitted.
  • Spamming: Spamming is not permitted.
  • Service Denial: DDoS or DoS activity is strictly prohibited.
  • Useless Calls: Misuse of assigned authority to call individuals for non-essential matters is not permitted.
  • Leave Without Notice: Leaving without notifying a XI-21 may result in questioning and possible consequences if important documents were held or malicious activity is suspected.
